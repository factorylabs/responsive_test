Amazing Courses, Amazing Simplicity

Creating courses in OpenClass takes just a few steps

  •  
       

    Create courses easily

    •   •   •  

    Just fill in a few details and your course is ready to be populated with content. You can even upload an existing course from Blackboard, Angel, or Moodle with a simple tool. Changing details about the course is just as easy.

  •  
       

    Add diverse content on the fly

    •   •   •  

    OpenClass is built to be open to all types of content. Bring in curated content from the OpenClass Exchange, link to other websites, or import content you've already created such as Word documents, PDFs, and videos.

  •  
       

    Shape content to match needs

    •   •   •  

    Create and edit different types of content, like discussion threads and content pages, without typing out a single line of code. Use the straightforward visual editor to create or adjust content so that it fits your course and looks exactly how you want it to. Or, dive right into the HTML and edit it directly.

  •  
       

    Manage courses all in one place

    •   •   •   •  

    Reorder content items or add them into your gradebook. Archive content you want to save for later or delete it completely, all in one place.

Learn Together, Learn Better

OpenClass is filled with many ways to communicate and collaborate

  •  
       

    All in one

    •   •   •  

    OpenClass gives students a single dashboard where they can do it all. From checking calendars and email to working together and submitting assignments, the dashboard makes learning simpler than ever before.

  •  
       

    Talk it out

    •   •   •  

    Students can use the Google chat and Skype features right in OpenClass to connect with educators and their peers. You don't even have to leave the course content.

  •  
       

    Start a conversation

    •   •   •  

    Both students and educators can create threaded discussions or post to activity feeds, giving you more freedom to have organic discussions about what you're learning.

  •  
       

    Make working together work better

    •   •  

    OpenClass has tools from Google that help you create, share, and edit all types of documents in the cloud. Make comments or revisions that everyone has access to instantly.

  •  
       

    Take your study group online

    •   •   •  

    Anyone can create their own groups to work and study the way they want to. Share resources, ideas, and learn better together.

  •  
       

    Learn socially

    •   •   •   •   •  

    Post your status, helpful resources or questions to your class or your university activity feeds. Create a profile, follow others, and build your network for a richer learning experience.

The OpenClass Exchange

Truly exceptional content and courses just a click away

  •  
       

    Browse a curated marketplace

    •   •  

    The upcoming OpenClass Exchange will feature the best content out there, all in one place. It will include curated OER resources, as well as content from educators, Pearson, and other publishers.

  •  
       

    Experience born digital content

    •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •  

    Digital copies of printed content are a thing of the past. The Exchange will have courses and content created from scratch to take advantage of the latest technology so they're more engaging, more intelligent, and more effective.

  •  
       

    Use great Open Educational Resources

    •   •   •   •  

    You can select from exceptional OER content like video and more from The Khan Academy, Ted-Ed, and YouTube EDU, along with others that use the Creative Commons license.

  •  
       

    Choose it and use it, right away

    •   •  

    The Exchange will let you preview content simply and quickly, then bring it into your course seamlessly.

See What They're Learning

OpenClass gives you visibility into students and course performance

  •  
       

    Make the grade, easily

    •  

    Now it's simple to assign grades, export them, and give feedback to one student or hundreds. And we made it easy to evaluate your students' performance and make changes to meet their individual needs.

  •  
       

    Know what's working (and what's not)

    •  

    Get detailed information on how students engage with your course so that you can personalize learning to their needs.

  •  
       

    Turn Big Data into a big difference

    •  

    With data from many students, you can get deep insight into performance that helps you optimize content and boost course efficacy.

  •  
       

    Keep students on track

    •  

    The At-Risk Student Dashboard connects the dots between assessments and course usage to tell you if students are falling behind before it's too late.

   

 

    

"When you get access to the data and analytics, you get insights into student behaviors and course efficacy, and then you can start optimizing content in real-time. This is the power of Big Data on OpenClass."

—Adrian Sannier, SVP of Product, OpenClass
